// 01 — Profile

About

Mechanical Engineering, BSc · Est. 2026
Dedan Kimathi University of Technology

I work at the intersection of mechanical design and the digital systems that increasingly run alongside it. My background spans aircraft maintenance on turboprop control surfaces, CNC fabrication, and most recently, CubeSat structural design at the Kenya Space Agency, where I modeled assemblies in SolidWorks and prepared them for 3D printing on a Bambu Lab printer.

Cybersecurity training came out of a simple observation: the machines I was building; CNC rigs, drones, autonomous systems which are also networked endpoints. I picked up ethical hacking and malware analysis fundamentals to understand that side, and to design systems that are secure from the first sketch, not patched afterward.

I'm fluent in French, English and Swahili, and I build small software tools on the side; including a flock-management app for poultry farmers and a handful of client websites.
